About WasteWise

WasteWise is an AI-powered waste management solution designed to help users identify, sort, and responsibly dispose of their daily waste. Our goal is to empower individuals and communities to make smarter, greener choices every day.

Our Mission

To reduce waste and promote recycling by making waste management easy, accessible, and educational for everyone.

Our Vision

A cleaner, greener planet where every individual contributes to sustainable living and environmental protection.

Why WasteWise?

We use technology and community-driven knowledge to help you make the best decisions for waste disposal and recycling.

How It Works

  1. Take a photo or upload an image: Use your phone or computer to capture your waste item.
  2. AI waste detection: Our system analyzes the image and identifies the waste category (plastic, organic, metal, etc.).
  3. Get disposal guidance: Receive step-by-step instructions for proper disposal or recycling based on your location.
  4. Track your impact: See how your actions contribute to a cleaner Earth and earn badges for eco-friendly habits!

  • Separate wet and dry waste daily for easier recycling.
  • Use compost bins for organic waste and create natural fertilizer for your plants.
  • Clean recyclables before disposing to avoid contamination.
  • Avoid plastic bags â€“ choose eco alternatives like cloth or jute bags!
